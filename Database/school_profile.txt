School Summary:
School Name: Crescent School
Location: 2365 Bayview Avenue, Toronto, Canada
Grades: 1-12
Type: Private Boys' School
Mission: Men of Character from Boys of Promise
School Values: Respect, Responsibility, Honesty, Compassion
Website: https://www.crescentschool.org/
One of the top independent schools for boys in Canada, Crescent School spans 37 acres in midtown Toronto. Now in our second century, we provide rich and diverse experiences and opportunities for boys in Grades 3–12 to learn, grow and thrive. Character has always been at the centre of a Crescent education supporting our mission, Men of Character from Boys of Promise.
